Computer Science

Computer Science is the study of algorithms, data structures, programming languages, and systems to create software and solve computational problems. Below are some areas of computer science offered by our university:

  • Software Development: Involves creating applications, software solutions, and systems for a variety of industries. This field focuses on coding, debugging, testing, and deployment.

    Courses for further studies in Software Development:
    - Software Engineering
    - Agile Development
    - Full Stack Development
    - DevOps Engineering
    - Cloud Computing
    - Mobile App Development

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning: Focuses on creating intelligent systems, algorithms, and models that can learn from data and make predictions or decisions.

    Popular postgraduate courses include:
    - Machine Learning and Deep Learning
    - Natural Language Processing
    - Computer Vision
    - Data Science and Analytics
    - AI in Robotics

  • Cybersecurity: Encompasses protecting computer systems and networks from cyber threats, breaches, and unauthorized access.

    Postgraduate courses in Cybersecurity include:
    - Cybersecurity Management
    - Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing
    - Information Security
    - Digital Forensics
    - Cyber Risk Management

  • Data Science: Deals with analyzing large volumes of data to extract insights and trends for decision-making and prediction.

    Courses for further studies in Data Science:
    - Data Analysis
    - Big Data Technologies
    - Data Mining
    - Data Visualization
    - Data-Driven Decision Making
