Business Studies

Business Studies focuses on understanding how organizations operate, how they are managed, and how they adapt to changing markets and environments. Here are some different types of business courses offered by our university:

  • Marketing Management: Involves promoting products and services, analyzing market trends, and creating advertising campaigns to attract and retain customers.

    Some subjects included in the Marketing Management course are:
    - Consumer Behavior
    - Advertising and Promotion
    - Digital Marketing
    - Sales Management
    - Marketing Strategy

  • Financial Management: Focuses on the management of financial resources, investment strategies, budgeting, and risk management.

    The Financial Management course includes topics like:
    - Corporate Finance
    - Investment Analysis
    - Financial Planning
    - Risk Management
    - Taxation

  • Human Resource Management: Deals with recruitment, training, employee relations, compensation, and organizational culture.

    The Human Resource Management course covers subjects such as:
    - Recruitment and Selection
    - Employee Training and Development
    - Compensation and Benefits
    - Labor Law and Industrial Relations
    - Organizational Behavior

  • Operations Management: Focuses on the design, plann ing, and control of business operations, including production processes, supply chain management, and quality control.

    The Operations Management course includes topics like:
    - Production Planning
    - Supply Chain Management
    - Quality Control
    - Lean Manufacturing
    - Project Management
