VELLORE INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a private deemed university located in Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1984, VIT has grown to become one of the premier engineering institutions in India.

VIT consistently ranks among the top engineering institutions in India by various ranking agencies like NIRF, QS World University Rankings, and Times Higher Education (THE). VIT is accredited by NAAC with an 'A' grade and by NBA for its engineering programs.

VIT boasts a vast network of alumni who have made significant contributions to various fields globally. Many alumni hold leadership positions in top companies and organizations worldwide.
